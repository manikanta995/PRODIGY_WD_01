About Us

Welcome to Coffee with Mani, where passion meets the perfect brew! We are a family-owned coffee roastery and café dedicated to sharing our love for coffee with the world. Our Story Coffee with Mani is the culmination of a lifelong dream and a shared passion for exceptional coffee. Founded in 2010 by Mani Patel, a coffee enthusiast and connoisseur, our journey began with a single coffee roaster and a small café in the heart of our hometown. Mani's vision was simple: to create a space where people could savor the artistry and flavors of freshly roasted coffee beans. Over the years, Coffee with Mani has evolved into a community hub for coffee lovers, where conversations flow as freely as the espresso, and every cup tells a story. Our Mission At Coffee with Mani, our mission is to source the finest coffee beans from around the world and handcraft them into exceptional blends. We believe that every bean has a unique story to tell, and it's our job to bring that story to life in every cup we serve. We are committed to: Quality: We meticulously select our coffee beans and roast them in small batches to ensure the highest quality and freshest taste. Sustainability: We are dedicated to ethical sourcing practices, supporting coffee-growing communities, and minimizing our environmental footprint. Community: Coffee is more than a beverage; it's a shared experience. We foster a welcoming and inclusive community where coffee lovers can come together to learn, connect, and enjoy exceptional coffee.
